QUARTZ

Based on grade

A grade(Snow) Quartz

Snow Quartz is a rare and exquisite variety of quartz, prized for its exceptional clarity and delicate beauty. This stunning mineral is characterized by:


- Crystal clear or white appearance, reminiscent of freshly fallen snow

- Inclusions of tiny air bubbles or wispy veils, adding to its ethereal charm

- High transparency and luster, giving it a sparkling appearance

- Unique snowflake-like patterns or wispy formations within the crystal


B grade(Super Semi) Quartz

 Super Semi Quartz is a high-quality quartz crystal that offers exceptional value and beauty. This stunning mineral boasts:


- High clarity with minimal inclusions, providing a clear channel for energy.

- A subtle, wispy, or veil-like appearance, adding to its unique charm.

- A range of colors, including crystal clear, white, pink, or smoky.

- A strong, sturdy structure, making it perfect for handling and display.

C grade(Semi) Quartz

"C Grade Semi Quartz is a beautiful and affordable quartz crystal that offers a unique blend of quality and value. This stunning mineral features:


- A more included or cloudy appearance, with visible imperfections

- A range of colors, including crystal clear, white, pink, or smoky

- A sturdy structure, making it perfect for handling and display

- A more rustic, natural beauty that adds to its charm

Sugar Quartz

Our Sugar Quartz is a type of quartz crystal known for its sparkling, sugar-like appearance. This unique mineral product features a cluster of small, white or colorless quartz crystals that resemble granulated sugar. Sugar Quartz is ideal for:

*Decorative and ornamental applications

*Healing and spiritual practices

Sugar Quartz is also known as "Milky Quartz" or "White Quartz" due to its appearance. It's a popular mineral among collectors and healers.

Granular Quartz

Our Granular Quartz is a high-purity, natural mineral product consisting of quartz grains with a uniform particle size distribution. This premium product is ideal for various industrial applications, including:


- Water filtration and purification systems

- Construction and building materials (e.g., concrete, mortar, and cement)

- Glass manufacturing and ceramics production

- Paints, coatings, and adhesives

- Electronic and optical applications

Rutilated quartz

Our Rutilated Quartz is a rare and delicate, golden needles of rutile crystals embedded within a crystal clear quartz matrix. This stunning combination creates a mesmerizing visual effect, reminiscent of a celestial landscape. Rutilated Quartz is ideal for

*Collectors and mineral enthusiasts

*Healing and spiritual practices

*High-end jewelry and accessory design

*Decorative and ornamental applications

Rutile Varieties:

*Golden Rutile (most common)

*Copper Rutile (reddish-orange)

*Silver Rutile (rare)

Mica infused Quartz

*Decorative and ornamental applications

*Glass factory

*Rubber factory

Key Characteristics:
